About This Web App

This web application, developed by Harold Trinidad, provides automated quality checking reminders for equipment owners and machine operators. By eliminating the need for manual timers or alarms, which can be easily overlooked, it ensures timely and consistent quality checks at specified intervals, enhancing operational efficiency and reducing the risk of missed inspections.

close

Instructions

1. Activating the Alarm:

  • Click the TAMU button to activate the alarm.
  • Upon activation, the button will turn red and display the message "TAMU is ON."
  • The alarm will sound every 15 minutes.

2. Deactivating the Alarm:

  • When the alarm sounds, click the TAMU button to deactivate it.
  • To reactivate the alarm, click the TAMU button again after turning it off.

3. Expiration Date Display:

  • The 180 Days and 210 Days buttons will display the corresponding expiration date.
  • These dates must be printed on the noodle pouch during the quality check process.
